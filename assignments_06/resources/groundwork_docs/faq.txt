Frequently Asked Questions

Hours
- Monday through Friday: 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM
- Saturday and Sunday: 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM
- We are closed on Thanksgiving Day and Christmas Day.

Locations
- Downtown: 42 Lexington Ave, Asheville, NC
- West Asheville: 800 Haywood Rd, Asheville, NC
- River Arts District: 175 Clingman Ave, Asheville, NC

Loyalty Program
The loyalty program is free to join. You earn one point for every dollar you spend. When you reach 100 points, you can redeem them for any free drink on the menu. Sign up at the register or on our website.

Gift Cards
Gift cards are available in any amount at the register or online.

Dog Policy
All three locations have a dog-friendly outdoor patio. Dogs are not permitted inside.

Wi-Fi
Free Wi-Fi is available at all locations. Ask a barista for the current password.
