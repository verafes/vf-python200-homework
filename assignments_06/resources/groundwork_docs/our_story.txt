Our Story

Groundwork Coffee Co. was founded in 2018 by two college friends, Maya Torres and Sam Okafor, in Asheville, North Carolina. Maya had spent two years working on a coffee farm in Guatemala. Sam had managed a community center in his hometown. They believed that good coffee and strong communities go together.

Their first shop opened with six tables, one espresso machine, and a commitment to sourcing only fair-trade, sustainably grown beans directly from small farms. They called it "Groundwork" because every strong community starts with the work you do on the ground — with your neighbors, your local farmers, and the people who walk through your door.

Today, Groundwork has three locations in Asheville. All beans are sourced directly from partner farms in Guatemala, Ethiopia, and Colombia. We pay above fair-trade prices, visit our partner farms once a year, and publish our sourcing reports publicly.

Our mission: to make great coffee that people can feel good about, in a space where everyone belongs.
