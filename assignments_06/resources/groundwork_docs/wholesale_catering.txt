Wholesale and Catering

Wholesale Coffee
We sell our house blends and single-origin beans in bulk to local restaurants, offices, and retailers. Wholesale pricing is available for orders of 5 pounds or more. We offer three roast profiles: light, medium, and dark.

To ask about wholesale pricing, email hello@groundworkcoffee.com with the subject line "Wholesale Inquiry."

Catering
We offer catering for events of 20 people or more. Our catering packages include:

- Coffee service (regular and decaf, with milk and sugar)
- Full bar setup (espresso drinks, cold brew, seasonal specials)
- Pastry trays (minimum order of 12 pastries)

Catering orders require at least 72 hours of advance notice. For events of 50 or more, we send a trained barista to operate the setup on-site.

To book catering, email hello@groundworkcoffee.com with your event date, location, estimated guest count, and preferred package.

Pricing varies by package and event size. We are happy to provide a quote.
